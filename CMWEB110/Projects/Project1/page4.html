My Hobbies

On this page, I'd like to talk about my hobbies!


One of my favorite ways to pass the time is by playing my Nintendo Switch.

Currently, I am playing The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening.
I really love the game so far as it is simple but very visually appealing and fun to play!

One of my favorite games of all time also happens to be on the Nintendo Switch.

Mario Odyssey is just an excellent game all around. Although it's challenging to 100% complete, I like the mechanics Nintendo used for Mario's controls compared to their past 3D Mario games.

I also enjoy watching speed runs of Mario Odyssey. I've never personally tried my hand at speed running, but maybe one day I will!

I also have a PC that I spend a lot of time on. For the most part I play games on it, but I also like to fine-tune and upgrade my hardware whenever I can.

I just got my desktop at the end of April and I've been loving it.

Even though I wanted to build my own PC, I had to buy a pre-built PC because of the insane graphics card shortage that's been happening since about December 2020.

For those who care, here are some of the specs:

  • My graphics card is a Gigabyte RTX 3060 with 12GB of VRAM
  • The processor is an Intel i7 11700F
  • I have 16GB of ram, but I'm looking to upgrade that to 32gb here soon
  • I have a 500GB SSD which I'd like to upgrade to 2TB, as well as a 1TB hard drive
  • For my peripherals I use the Logitech lightspeed wireless keyboard (G915) and mouse (G305) and a curved 27" Dell monitor (S2721HGF)

I mostly play games like Valorant and Rainbow Six Siege, but I've been playing a lot of Rocket League lately.

Other than playing video games, I also have a girlfriend, Olivia, who currently lives in Columbia, Missouri, for school.

I try to visit her as often as possible, but sometimes we are both too busy and have to go a while without seeing each other.

Although it's tough living so far away, we're hoping to live closer together once she graduates next school year and begins grad school.

For the most part, I spend my days either working, playing video games, working on school work, or visiting my girlfriend when I can.

I also enjoy skateboarding, frisbee golfing, hiking, and occasionally kayaking.

That's all for my hobbies as well as this project!
I hope you could get a good sense of who I am and some of my interests!
